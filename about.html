Our Story

White Label Fragrances began as a small handcrafted fragrance studio focused on helping people feel confident, beautiful, and calm through luxurious scent and skincare rituals.

Mission

To create premium, accessible beauty and fragrance products that elevate daily self-care and celebrate individuality.

Vision

To become a leading modern beauty lifestyle brand known for elegant formulas, meaningful scent stories, and customer trust.

Founder Information

Founded by Leila Monroe, White Label Fragrances was inspired by the emotional power of scent and skincare. Leila built the brand to blend science, artistry, and wellness into one refined experience.

Brand Values

  • Elegance and premium quality
  • Customer-first service
  • Inclusivity and confidence
  • Responsible ingredient selection

Customer Promise

Every order delivers a luxury experience: beautifully designed products, transparent quality standards, and a caring support team committed to your wellbeing.
